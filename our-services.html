Our Services

At Stella Maris we pride ourselves on assisting seafarers in a number of different ways, here are some of our services

Free WiFi

Stella Maris offers free wifi to all of our guests. We aim to make it as easy as possible for anyone to be able to easily contact love ones.

Money Exhange

Affordable money exchange is an essential service that we at Stella Maris offer. It is extremely important to give seafarers the best possible rate so they can purchase their essentials.

Food and Drinks

Our guests will have access to food and drinks, which they will be able to enjoy while relaxing in our comfortable establishment.

Sim Cards

Contacting loved ones is an essential right that all should be able to access. Stella Maris sells sim cards at affordable prices so that all seafarers can call home.
