Volunteering at Stella Maris

Men and women who volunteer their time work for various amounts of time. Some give one evening per week, others a full day, others a morning or an afternoon.

They can be employed in any or all of the following tasks:

  • driving the Stella Maris bus to pick up seafarers;

  • talking to and entertaining seafarers;

  • cleaning the Centre;

  • helping crew with sim cards and recharge cards;

  • selling drinks, snacks and confectionery.

A few more experienced volunteers visit the ships and go on board.

If you would like to volunteer please call 9430-8488 or email stella.maris@perthcatholic.org.au

Donate Now

Seafarers spend most of their life at sea and are unable to access stores easily. Any donations are welcome, from clothing to magazines, to games or any lose change.
